Medellin

Raft Rioverde and Samana river, a clear green river that carves through untouched Colombian rainforest. Start the adventure in Medellin and drive for 2 hours to San Francisco Municipallity, via Medellin-Bogota highway. After we drive 40 minutes until Boqueron and then we start a 40 minutes class 3 hiking until river through an amazin canyon, once in the river, we prepare the boats for the raft in this cristalline river for 5 hours with class III/IV rapids and beautiful waterfalls. We make a stop for taking lunch and enjoy the river, then we continue with the rafting activity until we arrive to our finish point in the bridge of Samana river. This rafting experience is beyond just paddling whitewater, it’s a fascinating journey into the heart of Colombia’s most untouched rainforest.

Bogota

You don’t have to go too far to enjoy the beauty of Mother Nature, just 1 hour away from Bogota there is one of the closest location where to experience spectacular water rafting activities. If you are with a group of minimum 6 persons, this can be done any day of the week. And if you are by yourself or less than 6 persons, the weekend is the best time to join an existing group lead by a certified instructor and the ride lasts around 90 minutes along this 7 km river. What you can expect during your visit: Hand sanitizer available to travelers and staff, Social distancing enforced throughout experience, Regularly sanitized high-traffic areas

Guatape

This activity is great for adrenaline junkies or thrill-seekers, it has the visit to the rock of El Peñol which is beautiful and then the rafting part that takes some serious 4x4 roading to get deep into the jungle to reach the river. Travelers will be briefed on rafting techniques, procedure, and safety to then start off the two and a half exciting journey down the Calderas river, all the time with English speaking guides.Traveler pickup is offered We do doorstep pickups in private vehicles with English speaking driver.
