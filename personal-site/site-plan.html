Overview

Purpose

I am a systems engineer, technology enthusiast, with more than 10 years of experience in programming, I have had the opportunity to work for several multinationals, in the development area; But when I entered the wonderful world of software, I did not know which way to go since there are many paths (Support, databases, infrastructure, back-end development, front-end development, etc.) and as the days go by the possibilities grow, that is why We want to help you understand the different areas better and help you make the best decision for your future. Here you will find programming courses in different programming languages, design patterns, good practices, but architecture as on-premises as cloud, databases, technological trends, etc., starting all from scratch, so that you can clearly understand and acquire the tools necessary to carry out your projects.

Audience

This site is aimed at technology enthusiasts, who want to enter the world of application development, who are not sure of the path they want to take and are looking for someone who can guide them in the vast world of software development. Application developers without much experience who want to find courses from scratch to advanced level, or for those expert developers who just want to be updated with the new tools they offer and technologies that are in demand in the world of work.

Content

Wolf

Technology advances at an accelerated pace and invasively penetrates each of the activities we carry out, whether in our jobs or when making a purchase, an electronic payment, interacting with an appliance or something simpler like sending a message from our mobile devices, information technologies are present and that is why being able to live with them is increasingly important and in the case of professionals it becomes a differentiating factor in the workplace.
Adjusting to this changing world is becoming increasingly important, it makes us more competitive in today's world of work, regardless of what career we study or the profession we pursue, it allows us to be more efficient, information technology provides us with tools that will make us simpler the processes that we carry out on a day-to-day basis, and even better protect our personal information and the information of our companies.

Images for the Home page

Disruptive change

The most recent health crisis transformed our daily lives, let us to understand that being productive does not attract us to an office, or that our place of residence prevents us from accessing different job or training offers, on the contrary, it showed us that we can make more appropriate use of our time from the comfort of home. This situation really altered our daily life, we tried not to have physical contact, which let us to use technological means to carry out tasks that we previously did in simpler ways, such as paying with contactless methods through our mobile devices, we resorted to make purchases through different applications or platforms and in this way continue with our lives in the most normal way possible, we limit our interactions with other people and although this is not emotionally healthy, it did show us that there were other ways of performing daily tasks ; the problem was that, but not all of us were prepared for this situation, which is why for many it was a disruptive change, which brought chaos to our lives, but in the midst of chaos many opportunities arise and no solution arises without first presenting a need , today it is a necessity to be able to know about technology to be able to take advantage of the benefits it brings to our lives.

Introduction to ICT

Here you will find from introductory courses to advanced courses in different branches of technology, you will find courses about personal information protection, computer security, office automation courses, programming in different languages and frameworks, software architecture, infrastructure, among other interesting topics.
We have qualified professionals, with extensive experience in different technological projects, as well as in other areas of knowledge, we firmly believe that it is from the diversity of thought that people manage to innovate, for this reason we believe that anyone can contribute a lot to the technological development and that if they learn to use information technology and make good use of it, they will have much more to contribute to society, so we want to make available to each one of them tools that allow them to break the limits and take their skills to another level, that they can share their knowledge with the world and apply it to the opportunities that this new technological normality has opened.
We know that many people resist change and even more so when there have been people who have misused technology and even abused it, but knowing more about technology, knowing how it works, gives us the opportunity to Accessing each of the benefits that technological growth brings to our lives is not an easy path, but at some point we all have to go through it, so welcome and see you on the road.
