Overview

Purpose

I am a systems engineer, technology enthusiast, with more than 10 years of experience in programming, I have had the opportunity to work for several multinationals, in the development area; But when I entered the wonderful world of software, I did not know which way to go since there are many paths (Support, databases, infrastructure, back-end development, front-end development, etc.) and as the days go by the possibilities grow, that is why We want to help you understand the different areas better and help you make the best decision for your future. Here you will find programming courses in different programming languages, design patterns, good practices, but architecture as on-premises as cloud, databases, technological trends, etc., starting all from scratch, so that you can clearly understand and acquire the tools necessary to carry out your projects.

Audience

This site is aimed at technology enthusiasts, who want to enter the world of application development, who are not sure of the path they want to take and are looking for someone who can guide them in the vast world of software development. Application developers without much experience who want to find courses from scratch to advanced level, or for those expert developers who just want to be updated with the new tools they offer and technologies that are in demand in the world of work.
