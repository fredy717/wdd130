Wolf


Technology advances at an accelerated pace and invasively penetrates each of the activities we carry out, whether in our jobs or when making a purchase, an electronic payment, interacting with an appliance or something simpler like sending a message from our mobile devices, information technologies are present and that is why being able to live with them is increasingly important and in the case of professionals it becomes a differentiating factor in the workplace.
Adjusting to this changing world is becoming increasingly important, it makes us more competitive in today's world of work, regardless of what career we study or the profession we pursue, it allows us to be more efficient, information technology provides us with tools that will make us simpler the processes that we carry out on a day-to-day basis, and even better protect our personal information and the information of our companies.


Disruptive change


The most recent health crisis transformed our daily lives, let us to understand that being productive does not attract us to an office, or that our place of residence prevents us from accessing different job or training offers, on the contrary, it showed us that we can make more appropriate use of our time from the comfort of home. This situation really altered our daily life, we tried not to have physical contact, which let us to use technological means to carry out tasks that we previously did in simpler ways, such as paying with contactless methods through our mobile devices, we resorted to make purchases through different applications or platforms and in this way continue with our lives in the most normal way possible, we limit our interactions with other people and although this is not emotionally healthy, it did show us that there were other ways of performing daily tasks ; the problem was that, but not all of us were prepared for this situation, which is why for many it was a disruptive change, which brought chaos to our lives, but in the midst of chaos many opportunities arise and no solution arises without first presenting a need , today it is a necessity to be able to know about technology to be able to take advantage of the benefits it brings to our lives.
